About

We at THE PHASING PHOENIX strive to provide a
calm and relaxing experience unlike any other.

We are a brand new, fledgling establishment made up of players from all walks of life, be it hardcore raiders or even curious people looking to dip their toes into XIV's RP scene for the very first time in their lives. The idea of The Phasing Phoenix started out as a joke being passed around among friends during the incredibly long and tedious content drought of Patch 5.3. Many of us were raiders, and at the time we playfully toyed with the idea of working in an RP bar to pass the time. To myself, I had thought that it would be incredibly ironic and funny if the idea actually became real. And so, I sought out to build my idea of a bar and lounge that my friends would also come to enjoy, relax and wind down, regardless of their preference or lack thereof for RP.

Lo and behold, The Phasing Phoenix was born.
